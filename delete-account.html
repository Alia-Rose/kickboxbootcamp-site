DELETE ACCOUNT & DATA

How to delete your Kickbox Bootcamp account or data

Delete Data Without Deleting Your Account

You can delete your workout data while keeping your account active:

  1. Open the app and tap the Settings tab
  2. Scroll to the Data Management section
  3. Tap Clear Workout History
  4. Confirm the deletion

This permanently deletes all recorded workout history, statistics, and insights data. Your account, settings, favorites, and exercise customizations are not affected.

You can also request partial data deletion by emailing us at smallempirestudiosofficial@gmail.com specifying which data you would like removed.

Delete Your Account in the App

You can delete your account directly from the Kickbox Bootcamp app by following these steps:

  1. Open the app and tap the Settings tab
  2. Tap Manage Account
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Enter your password to confirm

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

smallempirestudiosofficial@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile (email, display name)
  • All workout history and statistics
  • Saved favorite workouts
  • Exercise customizations (frequency and rep adjustments)
  • Device sessions and authentication tokens
  • Referral data

What Data Is Retained

  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Subscription and payment records managed by Google Play are subject to Google's data retention policies

Deletion Timeline

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have an active Pro subscription, it will be cancelled but no refund will be issued for the remaining period. Please manage your subscription through Google Play before deleting your account.
